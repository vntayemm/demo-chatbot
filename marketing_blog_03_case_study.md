# BLOG 03 - CASE STUDY: TU HOI DAP THU CONG DEN CHATBOT VAN HANH ON DINH

## Bai toan

Mot doi ngu sales/CS mat nhieu thoi gian tra loi cau hoi lap lai:
- Goi gia nao phu hop?
- Chinh sach uu dai ra sao?
- Huong dan thao tac co ban o dau?

Ket qua:
- Thoi gian phan hoi cham
- Noi dung khong dong nhat giua nhan su
- Kho do hieu qua

## Cach trien khai

Doanh nghiep trien khai chatbot theo 3 buoc:
1. Chuan hoa corpus theo domain `price` va `guide`
2. Dua vao bo governance: UAT, go-live, SLO/SLA, runbook
3. Theo doi KPI hang tuan va cai tien theo feedback

## Ket qua dau ky (tham chieu)

- Giam cau hoi lap lai cho team van hanh
- Tang toc do phan hoi cau hoi co ban
- Co du lieu KPI de bao cao minh bach

## Bai hoc rut ra

- Chatbot khong thay the quy trinh, chatbot can quy trinh
- Corpus la "nhien lieu", governance la "day an toan"
- Khong co sign-off ro rang thi kho mo rong

## Ket luan

Gia tri that su cua chatbot doanh nghiep la kha nang van hanh ben vung, khong chi la man demo.

**CTA:** Dat lich workshop 90 phut de xay lo trinh "demo -> production" cho don vi ban.
