# CUSTOMER COMPLIANCE - SLIDE READY (VI-EN)

## Slide 1 - Van de cua doanh nghiep / Enterprise Problem

- **VI:** Chatbot demo chay duoc, nhung kho dua vao production an toan.
- **EN:** Many chatbots work in demo, but fail to meet safe production standards.
- **VI:** Khach hang can bang chung van hanh, khong chi tinh nang.
- **EN:** Customers require operational evidence, not only feature demos.

## Slide 2 - Giai phap de xuat / Proposed Solution

- **VI:** Trien khai chatbot kem khung governance va operations readiness.
- **EN:** Deploy chatbot with built-in governance and operations readiness.
- **VI:** Chuan hoa UAT, go-live, risk, SLA/SLO, va incident response.
- **EN:** Standardize UAT, go-live, risk, SLA/SLO, and incident response.

## Slide 3 - Khung chuan tham chieu / Reference Frameworks

- **VI/EN:** ITIL/ITSM practices (incident, release readiness)
- **VI/EN:** SRE practices (SLI/SLO/SLA, error budget)
- **VI/EN:** ISO 27001/SOC 2 control intent (security, logging, auditability)
- **VI/EN:** ISO 31000 risk management intent

## Slide 4 - Van de duoc giai quyet / Problems Solved

- **VI:** Giam rui ro go-live bang checklist + rollback criteria.  
  **EN:** Reduce go-live risk via checklists and rollback criteria.
- **VI:** Rut ngan MTTR bang runbook P1/P2/P3.  
  **EN:** Shorten MTTR with P1/P2/P3 incident runbooks.
- **VI:** Dong nhat nghiem thu bang UAT pass/fail criteria.  
  **EN:** Standardize acceptance with clear UAT pass/fail criteria.

## Slide 5 - Cam ket van hanh / Operational Commitments

- **VI:** Moi release production deu qua UAT va go-live gate.
- **EN:** Every production release passes UAT and go-live gates.
- **VI:** Co SLA/SLO ro rang cho availability, latency, error rate.
- **EN:** Clear SLA/SLO targets for availability, latency, and error rate.

## Slide 6 - KPI production de xuat / Proposed Production KPIs

- **VI/EN:** Uptime >= 99.9% / month
- **VI/EN:** API 5xx error rate <= 1%
- **VI/EN:** p95 latency <= 2.0s (at designed load)
- **VI/EN:** Context-grounded answer rate >= 85%

## Slide 7 - Gia tri cho khach hang / Customer Value

- **VI:** Giam rui ro khi dua chatbot vao quy trinh thuc te.
- **EN:** Lower risk when moving chatbot into real business workflows.
- **VI:** Tang minh bach trong van hanh, audit, va su co.
- **EN:** Improve transparency in operations, audits, and incidents.

## Slide 8 - Xu huong thi truong / Market Trend

- **VI:** Enterprise AI dang chuyen sang governance-first.
- **EN:** Enterprise AI is shifting to governance-first adoption.
- **VI:** Security-by-design va auditability la yeu cau mua sam.
- **EN:** Security-by-design and auditability are procurement requirements.

## Slide 9 - Thong diep chot / Closing Message

- **VI:** Day khong chi la chatbot, day la khung van hanh production-ready.
- **EN:** This is not just a chatbot, it is a production-ready operating model.
