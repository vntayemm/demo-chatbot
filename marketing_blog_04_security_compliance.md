# BLOG 04 - BAO MAT VA COMPLIANCE CHO CHATBOT DOANH NGHIEP

## Mo dau

Chatbot xu ly du lieu nghiep vu, vi vay bao mat va compliance khong the de sau.  
Neu bo qua, rui ro lon nhat khong nam o model, ma nam o van hanh.

## 5 lop bao mat can co

1. **Transport security**: bat TLS/HTTPS
2. **Authentication/Authorization**: JWT/OAuth2/SSO + RBAC
3. **Data protection**: khong log token/PII, quan ly secret an toan
4. **Auditability**: co audit log va truy vet su kien
5. **Incident response**: runbook P1/P2/P3 + RCA

## Compliance readiness nghia la gi?

Khong nhat thiet phai co chung nhan ngay, nhung can:
- map duoc control intent
- co tai lieu va bang chung van hanh
- co quy trinh review dinh ky

## Sai lam pho bien

- Chi tap trung demo ma bo qua operations controls
- Khong co owner cho risk va security actions
- Khong co sign-off truoc go-live

## Ket luan

Bao mat la mot phan cua kien truc, khong phai buoc bo sung sau cung.

**CTA:** Nhan gap-analysis checklist de danh gia nhanh maturity bao mat chatbot cua ban.
