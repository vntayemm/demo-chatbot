    BAO GIA RUT GON 1 TRANG (VI-EN) - USD REFERENCE¶

    1. Ty gia tham chieu / Exchange Rate Reference¶

    • VI: Ty gia quy doi tham chieu: 1 USD = 25,000 VND
    • EN: Reference exchange rate: 1 USD = 25,000 VND

    VI: Gia USD chi de tham khao, bao gia chinh thuc thanh toan bang VND neu khong co thoa thuan khac.
    EN: USD values are for reference only. Official quotation and payment are in VND unless otherwise agreed.

    Goi / Package Thoi gian / Timeline Gia VND / Price (VND) USD tham khao / Reference USD

    Goi / Plan Gia VND / Price (VND per month) USD tham khao / Reference USD per month
    Basic support 25,000,000 1,000
    Advanced support 45,000,000 1,800

    4. Dieu khoan thuong mai / Commercial Terms¶

    • VI: Gia tren chua bao gom VAT.
      EN: Pricing is exclusive of VAT.

    • VI: Thanh toan de xuat: 40% ky hop dong - 40% sau UAT - 20% khi go-live/ban giao.
      EN: Proposed payment terms: 40% at contract signing - 40% after UAT - 20% at go-live/handover.

    • VI: Hieu luc bao gia: 30 ngay ke tu ngay phat hanh.
      EN: Quotation validity: 30 days from issuance date.

    5. Ghi chu pham vi / Scope Note¶

    • VI: Bao gia ap dung cho Price bot + Guide bot kem bo khung governance/operations readiness.
      EN: Pricing applies to Price bot + Guide bot with governance/operations readiness package.

    • VI: Cac tich hop dac thu, ha tang, license ben thu ba, va phi chung nhan compliance chinh thuc khong nam trong gia tren.
      EN: Custom integrations, infrastructure, third-party licenses, and formal certification costs are excluded.
