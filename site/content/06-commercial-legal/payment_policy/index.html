    PAYMENT POLICY (CHINH SACH THANH TOAN) - DEMO-CHATBOT¶

    1. Muc dich¶

    Chinh sach nay quy dinh nguyen tac thanh toan cho cac dich vu lien quan den demo-chatbot, bao gom: - Dich vu trien khai mot lan - Dich vu van hanh/bao tri dinh ky (neu co) - Cac thay doi pham vi (Change Request)

    2. Pham vi ap dung¶

    Ap dung cho: - Bao gia, hop dong, phu luc dich vu - Khach hang to chuc/doanh nghiep su dung dich vu

    Trong truong hop mau thuan giua chinh sach nay va hop dong da ky, uu tien theo hop dong da ky.

    3. Dong tien va thue¶

    • Don vi tien te mac dinh: VND
    • Cac gia tri USD (neu co) chi mang tinh tham khao, tru khi hop dong quy dinh khac.
    • Gia tri dich vu [da/chua] bao gom VAT theo bao gia/hop dong tung thoi diem.

    4. Nguyen tac thanh toan de xuat¶

    4.1 Dich vu trien khai mot lan¶

    Lich thanh toan tham chieu: - Dot 1: 40% khi ky hop dong - Dot 2: 40% sau UAT sign-off - Dot 3: 20% sau Go-live/ban giao

    4.2 Dich vu van hanh dinh ky (neu co)¶

    • Thanh toan theo chu ky thang/quy theo hop dong.
    • Han thanh toan: trong [x] ngay lam viec ke tu ngay xuat hoa don.

    4.3 Dich vu thay doi pham vi (CR)¶

    • Moi CR duoc bao gia rieng.
    • Co the ap dung thanh toan 50/50 hoac theo milestone cua CR.

    5. Phuong thuc thanh toan¶

    • Chuyen khoan vao tai khoan da dang ky cua ben cung cap.
    • Noi dung chuyen khoan can ghi ro:
    • So hop dong/so hoa don
    • Ten khach hang
    • Dot thanh toan

    6. Hoa don va chung tu¶

    • Ben cung cap xuat hoa don/chung tu theo quy dinh phap luat hien hanh.
    • Khach hang co trach nhiem cung cap thong tin xuat hoa don chinh xac.
    • Sai sot thong tin xuat hoa don do ben nao cung cap sai, ben do chiu trach nhiem phoi hop dieu chinh.

    7. Cham thanh toan¶

    Neu qua han thanh toan: - Ben cung cap co quyen gui thong bao nhac thanh toan. - Sau [x] ngay qua han, ben cung cap co quyen tam ngung mot phan hoac toan bo dich vu (sau khi thong bao bang van ban), tru khi co thoa thuan khac. - Phi cham thanh toan (neu ap dung) duoc quy dinh cu the trong hop dong.

    8. Hoan phi va huy dich vu¶

    8.1 Nguyen tac chung¶

    • Cac khoan da thanh toan cho khoi luong da thuc hien hop le thuong khong hoan lai.
    • Hoan phi (neu co) duoc xac dinh dua tren:
    • Khoi luong cong viec da hoan thanh
    • Chi phi da phat sinh
    • Thoa thuan trong hop dong

    8.2 Truong hop huy tu phia khach hang¶

    • Khach hang thong bao huy bang van ban.
    • Hai ben doi soat khoi luong da thuc hien de quyet toan.

    8.3 Truong hop huy tu phia nha cung cap¶

    • Ben cung cap thong bao ly do va de xuat phuong an xu ly:
    • Tiep tuc voi ke hoach dieu chinh, hoac
    • Quyết toan khoi luong va hoan tra phan chua thuc hien (neu co)

    9. Dieu chinh gia¶

    Gia dich vu co the duoc dieu chinh trong cac truong hop: - Thay doi pham vi nghiep vu - Thay doi yeu cau ky thuat/bao mat ngoai pham vi ban dau - Thay doi phap ly/thue phi theo quy dinh moi

    Moi dieu chinh gia tri phai duoc xac nhan bang van ban (phu luc/CR).
