    1. Goi A - Foundation¶

    Subject goi y / Suggested subject:
    [Quotation - Package A] Chatbot Foundation for [Company Name]

    Noi dung mail / Email body:

    Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],
    Dear [Name],

    Dua tren nhu cau trien khai nhanh de kiem chung hieu qua chatbot, ben em de xuat Goi A - Foundation.
    Based on your need for fast validation, we propose Package A - Foundation.

    Pham vi chinh / Scope: - Trien khai Price bot + Guide bot co ban
    Core deployment of Price bot + Guide bot - UAT va go-live checklist
    UAT and go-live checklist - Ban giao de su dung noi bo va test nghiep vu
    Handover for internal usage and business testing

    Thong tin tom tat / Summary: - Timeline: 2-3 weeks - Price: 120,000,000 VND (VAT excluded)

    Tai lieu dinh kem / Attachments: - bao_gia_rut_gon_1_trang_bilingual.md

    Neu dong y, ben em de xuat buoi hop 30-45 phut de chot scope va kickoff.
    If aligned, we propose a 30-45 minute call to finalize scope and kickoff.

    Tran trong / Best regards,
    [Ten ban / Your Name]

    2. Goi B - Professional¶

    Subject goi y / Suggested subject:
    [Quotation - Package B] Chatbot Professional for [Company Name]

    Noi dung mail / Email body:

    Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],
    Dear [Name],

    Voi muc tieu van hanh chatbot on dinh trong noi bo, ben em de xuat Goi B - Professional.
    For stable internal operations, we propose Package B - Professional.

    Pham vi chinh / Scope: - Toan bo noi dung Goi A
    Full scope of Package A - Bo sung governance va operations docs: risk, SLA/SLO, incident runbook
    Added governance/operations docs: risk, SLA/SLO, incident runbook - Benchmark co ban + onboarding team van hanh
    Baseline benchmark + operations onboarding

    Thong tin tom tat / Summary: - Timeline: 4-6 weeks - Price: 240,000,000 VND (VAT excluded)

    Tai lieu dinh kem / Attachments: - bao_gia_rut_gon_1_trang_bilingual.md - customer_compliance_statement_bilingual.md

    Neu phu hop, ben em de xuat buoi scope 60 phut de chot KPI UAT/go-live.
    If suitable, we suggest a 60-minute scoping session to finalize UAT/go-live KPIs.

    Tran trong / Best regards,
    [Ten ban / Your Name]

    3. Goi C - Enterprise Readiness¶

    Subject goi y / Suggested subject:
    [Quotation - Package C] Enterprise-Ready Chatbot for [Company Name]

    Noi dung mail / Email body:

    Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],
    Dear [Name],

    Neu muc tieu la production scale voi governance/reliability cao, ben em de xuat Goi C - Enterprise Readiness.
    If your target is production scale with stronger governance/reliability, we recommend Package C - Enterprise Readiness.

    Pham vi chinh / Scope: - Toan bo Goi B
    Full scope of Package B - Hardening production (auth, RBAC baseline, audit logging baseline)
    Production hardening (auth, RBAC baseline, audit logging baseline) - Compliance readiness theo control intent ISO 27001/SOC 2
    Compliance readiness aligned with ISO 27001/SOC 2 control intent - Go-live support + 2-week hypercare
    Go-live support + 2-week hypercare

    Thong tin tom tat / Summary: - Timeline: 8-10 weeks - Price: 420,000,000 VND (VAT excluded)

    Tai lieu dinh kem / Attachments: - bao_gia_de_xuat.md - customer_compliance_statement_bilingual.md - slo_sla.md

    Ben em de xuat buoi hop 60-90 phut voi business + technical + security team de chot scope chi tiet.
    We propose a 60-90 minute session with business, technical, and security teams to finalize detailed scope.

    Tran trong / Best regards,
    [Ten ban / Your Name]
