    Hai ben thong nhat ky ket hop dong voi noi dung chinh sau:

    Dieu 1. Doi tuong va pham vi¶

    Ben B cung cap dich vu trien khai chatbot production-ready cho Ben A, bao gom: - Trien khai Price bot + Guide bot theo pham vi da thong nhat; - Ban giao tai lieu van hanh theo goi dich vu (UAT checklist, go-live checklist, risk register, SLA/SLO, runbook incident); - Ho tro nghiem thu va ban giao theo milestone.

    Chi tiet pham vi va san pham ban giao duoc quy dinh tai Phu luc 01 (Scope of Work).

    Dieu 2. Tien do¶

    • Tong thoi gian du kien: [SO_TUAN] tuan, bat dau tu ngay [NGAY_BAT_DAU].
    • Tien do chi tiet theo Phu luc 02 (Milestones).
    • Thay doi pham vi/tien do chi co hieu luc khi co xac nhan bang van ban cua hai ben.

    Dieu 3. Gia tri hop dong va thanh toan¶

    • Tong gia tri hop dong: [SO_TIEN] VND (Bang chu: [BANG_CHU]).
    • Gia tri tren [chua/da] bao gom VAT.

    Lich thanh toan: - Dot 1: 40% khi ky hop dong; - Dot 2: 40% sau khi hoan tat UAT; - Dot 3: 20% sau khi go-live/ban giao.

    Dieu 4. Nghiem thu va ban giao¶

    • Tieu chi nghiem thu theo Phu luc 03 (Acceptance Criteria).
    • Ben A phan hoi ket qua nghiem thu trong [SO_NGAY] ngay lam viec ke tu khi nhan thong bao nghiem thu.
    • Neu Ben A khong phan hoi dung han, phan ban giao duoc xem la tam chap nhan.

    Dieu 5. Quyen va nghia vu cua cac ben¶

    5.1 Ben A¶

    • Cung cap thong tin, dau moi nghiep vu, du lieu va phoi hop kiem thu dung tien do.
    • Thanh toan day du, dung han theo Dieu 3.

    5.2 Ben B¶

    • Thuc hien dung pham vi, tien do va chat luong theo hop dong.
    • Khac phuc loi phat sinh trong pham vi da nghiem thu theo Dieu 7.

    Dieu 6. Bao mat va so huu tri tue¶

    • Hai ben cam ket bao mat thong tin trao doi trong qua trinh thuc hien hop dong.
    • Quyen so huu/su dung tai lieu, ma nguon, cau hinh va san pham ban giao duoc quy dinh tai Phu luc 04 (IP & License).

    Dieu 7. Bao hanh va ho tro¶

    • Thoi gian bao hanh ky thuat: [SO_THANG] thang ke tu ngay ky bien ban ban giao tong the.
    • Bao hanh ap dung cho loi trong pham vi da nghiem thu.
    • Yeu cau ngoai pham vi duoc xu ly theo co che Change Request.

    Dieu 8. Vi pham, cham dut, va tranh chap¶

    • Ben vi pham nghia vu co trach nhiem khac phuc va boi thuong thiet hai thuc te (neu co).
    • Hop dong co the tam ngung/cham dut neu mot ben vi pham nghiem trong va khong khac phuc trong [SO_NGAY] ngay ke tu khi nhan thong bao.
    • Tranh chap duoc uu tien giai quyet bang thuong luong; neu khong thanh, dua ra Toa an co tham quyen tai [DIA_DIEM].

    Dieu 9. Hieu luc hop dong¶

    • Hop dong co hieu luc ke tu ngay ky den khi cac ben hoan tat toan bo nghia vu.
    • Hop dong lap thanh [SO_BAN] ban co gia tri phap ly nhu nhau, moi ben giu [SO_BAN_MOI_BEN] ban.
