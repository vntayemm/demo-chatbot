    BAO GIA DE XUAT - Enterprise Chatbot | AI¶

    1. Thong tin chung¶

    • Khach hang:
    • Don vi cung cap:
    • Phien ban bao gia: v1.0
    • Ngay bao gia:
    • Hieu luc bao gia: 30 ngay ke tu ngay phat hanh

    2. Muc tieu pham vi¶

    Bao gia nay ap dung cho goi trien khai chatbot theo huong production-ready, bao gom: - Xay dung/hoan thien chatbot price bot va guide bot - Bo tai lieu governance va operations (UAT, go-live, risk, SLA/SLO, runbook) - Ho tro deploy staging/production co huong dan van hanh

    3. Cac goi de xuat¶

    Goi A - Foundation¶

    • Phu hop: PoC nhanh, quy mo nho
    • Pham vi:
    • Cai dat backend/frontend co ban
    • Tich hop 2 bot (price, guide)
    • Bo tai lieu cot loi: uat_checklist, go_live_checklist
    • Thoi gian: 2-3 tuan
    • Bao gia: 120,000,000 VND

    Goi B - Professional¶

    • Phu hop: Trien khai nghiem tuc cho don vi van hanh noi bo
    • Pham vi:
    • Toan bo Goi A
    • Them risk_register, slo_sla, runbook_incident
    • Benchmark co ban (latency/error/quality)
    • Chuyen giao onboarding cho team van hanh
    • Thoi gian: 4-6 tuan
    • Bao gia: 240,000,000 VND

    Goi C - Enterprise Readiness¶

    • Phu hop: Don vi can muc governance cao va san sang mo rong
    • Pham vi:
    • Toan bo Goi B
    • Hardening production (auth, RBAC, audit logging baseline)
    • Tu van readiness theo control intent ISO 27001/SOC 2
    • Ho tro go-live + hypercare 2 tuan
    • Thoi gian: 8-10 tuan
    • Bao gia: 420,000,000 VND

    4. Dich vu van hanh hang thang (tuy chon)¶

    • Goi Monitoring & Support co ban: 25,000,000 VND/thang
    • Theo doi SLO co ban, ho tro su co P2/P3 gio hanh chinh
    • Goi Monitoring & Support nang cao: 45,000,000 VND/thang
    • Theo doi 24/7, ho tro P1/P2/P3, review rui ro va KPI hang thang

    5. Bang tom tat chi phi¶

    Hang muc Goi A Goi B Goi C
    Trien khai mot lan 120,000,000 240,000,000 420,000,000
    Van hanh co ban/thang (tuy chon) 25,000,000 25,000,000 25,000,000
    Van hanh nang cao/thang (tuy chon) 45,000,000 45,000,000 45,000,000

    6. Dieu khoan thuong mai¶

    • Gia tren chua bao gom VAT.
    • Thanh toan de xuat:
    • 40% khi ky hop dong
    • 40% khi hoan tat UAT
    • 20% khi go-live/ban giao
    • Yeu cau thay doi pham vi se duoc danh gia va bao gia bo sung.

    7. Gia dinh va pham vi khong bao gom¶

    • Khach hang cung cap dau moi nghiep vu va phe duyet noi dung corpus.
    • Khong bao gom chi phi ha tang cloud/on-prem (server, database, monitoring tool license).
    • Khong bao gom phi chung nhan compliance chinh thuc boi ben thu ba.
    • Tich hop voi he thong ben thu ba se danh gia rieng neu co API dac thu.

    8. Lo trinh trien khai de xuat¶

    • Tuan 1: Kickoff, chot pham vi, chuan hoa corpus
    • Tuan 2-3: Trien khai va test chuc nang
    • Tuan 4: UAT va fix
    • Tuan 5+: Go-live va hypercare (tuy theo goi)
