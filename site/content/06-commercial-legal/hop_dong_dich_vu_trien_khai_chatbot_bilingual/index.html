    HOP DONG DICH VU TRIEN KHAI CHATBOT (VI-EN TEMPLATE)¶

    So hop dong / Contract No.: [SO_HD]/[YEAR]¶

    Hom nay, ngay [NGAY] thang [THANG] nam [NAM], tai [DIA_DIEM], cac ben gom:
    This contract is made on [DATE] at [LOCATION] by and between:

    Ben A / Party A (Customer)¶

    • Ten don vi / Legal entity:
    • Dai dien / Representative:
    • Chuc vu / Title:
    • Dia chi / Address:
    • Ma so thue / Tax code:
    • Dien thoai / Phone:
    • Email:

    Ben B / Party B (Service Provider)¶

    • Ten don vi / Legal entity:
    • Dai dien / Representative:
    • Chuc vu / Title:
    • Dia chi / Address:
    • Ma so thue / Tax code:
    • Dien thoai / Phone:
    • Email:

    Hai ben thong nhat cac dieu khoan sau:
    The parties agree to the following terms:

    Dieu 1. Doi tuong hop dong / Contract Scope¶

    VI: Ben B cung cap dich vu trien khai chatbot production-ready cho Ben A, bao gom trien khai Price bot, Guide bot, va bo tai lieu van hanh theo goi dich vu da thoa thuan.
    EN: Party B shall provide production-ready chatbot implementation services to Party A, including Price bot, Guide bot, and the agreed operations documentation package.

    Dieu 2. Pham vi cong viec va ban giao / Scope of Work and Deliverables¶

    VI: Pham vi chi tiet va ket qua ban giao duoc quy dinh tai Phu luc 01 (Scope of Work).
    EN: Detailed scope and deliverables are defined in Appendix 01 (Scope of Work).

    San pham ban giao co the bao gom / Deliverables may include: - Ma nguon/cau hinh theo pham vi / Source code and configuration within agreed scope - Tai lieu van hanh / Operations documentation - Bien ban nghiem thu va ban giao / Acceptance and handover records

    Dieu 3. Tien do thuc hien / Timeline¶

    VI: Tong thoi gian du kien: [SO_TUAN] tuan, bat dau tu [NGAY_BAT_DAU].
    EN: Estimated duration: [NUMBER_OF_WEEKS] weeks, starting from [START_DATE].

    VI: Tien do chi tiet theo Phu luc 02 (Milestones).
    EN: Detailed milestones are provided in Appendix 02.

    Dieu 4. Gia tri hop dong va thanh toan / Contract Value and Payment Terms¶

    • Tong gia tri hop dong / Total contract value: [SO_TIEN] VND
    • VAT / Tax: [Included/Excluded]

    Lich thanh toan / Payment schedule: - Dot 1 / Milestone 1: 40% khi ky hop dong / upon contract signing - Dot 2 / Milestone 2: 40% sau UAT / after UAT completion - Dot 3 / Milestone 3: 20% khi go-live/ban giao / upon go-live and handover

    Dieu 5. Nghiem thu / Acceptance¶

    VI: Tieu chi nghiem thu theo Phu luc 03 (UAT/Acceptance Criteria).
    EN: Acceptance criteria are defined in Appendix 03 (UAT/Acceptance Criteria).

    VI: Ben A phan hoi ket qua nghiem thu trong [SO_NGAY] ngay lam viec.
    EN: Party A shall provide acceptance feedback within [NUMBER_OF_DAYS] business days.

    Dieu 6. Bao mat / Confidentiality¶

    VI: Hai ben cam ket bao mat thong tin va du lieu lien quan den hop dong, khong tiet lo cho ben thu ba neu khong co chap thuan bang van ban, tru truong hop theo quy dinh phap luat.
    EN: Both parties shall keep all contract-related information and data confidential and shall not disclose to third parties without written consent, except where required by law.

    Dieu 7. So huu tri tue / Intellectual Property¶

    VI: Quyen so huu va quyen su dung san pham ban giao duoc quy dinh tai Phu luc 04 (IP Ownership & License).
    EN: Ownership and usage rights of deliverables are governed by Appendix 04 (IP Ownership & License).

    Dieu 8. Bao hanh va ho tro / Warranty and Support¶

    VI: Thoi gian bao hanh ky thuat: [SO_THANG] thang ke tu ngay ban giao tong the; ap dung cho loi trong pham vi da nghiem thu.
    EN: Technical warranty period: [NUMBER_OF_MONTHS] months from final handover; applicable to defects within accepted scope.

    Dieu 9. Thay doi pham vi / Change Request¶

    VI: Moi thay doi pham vi, chi phi, tien do phai duoc xac nhan bang van ban co chu ky hop le cua hai ben.
    EN: Any change in scope, cost, or timeline must be documented and signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

    Dieu 10. Vi pham, cham dut, tranh chap / Breach, Termination, Dispute Resolution¶

    VI: Neu mot ben vi pham nghiem trong nghia vu va khong khac phuc trong [SO_NGAY] ngay sau thong bao, ben con lai co quyen tam ngung/cham dut hop dong.
    EN: If a party materially breaches its obligations and fails to cure within [NUMBER_OF_DAYS] days after notice, the other party may suspend/terminate the contract.

    VI: Tranh chap duoc uu tien giai quyet bang thuong luong, neu khong thanh se dua ra Toa an co tham quyen tai [DIA_DIEM].
    EN: Disputes shall first be settled by negotiation; failing that, disputes shall be submitted to a competent court in [LOCATION].

    Dieu 11. Hieu luc hop dong / Effective Date¶

    VI: Hop dong co hieu luc tu ngay ky va het hieu luc khi cac ben hoan thanh nghia vu.
    EN: This contract takes effect from the signing date and remains valid until all obligations are fulfilled.

    DAI DIEN BEN A / FOR PARTY A DAI DIEN BEN B / FOR PARTY B
    (Ky, ghi ro ho ten, dong dau) (Ky, ghi ro ho ten, dong dau)

    • Phu luc 04 / Appendix 04: IP Ownership and License
    • Phu luc 05 / Appendix 05: SLA/SLO and Support Scope (if applicable)

    Luu y / Note: Mau nay mang tinh tham khao thuong mai. Truoc khi ky chinh thuc, de nghi duoc bo phan phap che/luat su cua cac ben ra soat.
