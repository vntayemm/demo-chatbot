    FAQ LIST - DEMO-CHATBOT¶

    1. Tong quan¶

    Danh sach cau hoi thuong gap de ho tro: - Nguoi dung cuoi - Team van hanh - Khach hang pre-sales

    2. FAQ cho nguoi dung¶

    Q1. Nen dung bot nao?¶

    A:
    - Dung Price bot khi hoi bang gia, so sanh goi, uu dai.
    - Dung Guide bot khi can huong dan cai dat/su dung.

    Q2. Vi sao chatbot tra loi chua dung y?¶

    A: Thu dat cau hoi cu the hon, bo sung boi canh (quy mo, muc tieu, dieu kien), hoac doi sang bot phu hop.

    Q3. Chatbot co thay the hoan toan nhan vien ho tro khong?¶

    A: Khong. Chatbot giam tai cau hoi lap lai, tang toc do phan hoi, va ho tro team.

    3. FAQ cho ky thuat/van hanh¶

    Q4. SLA/SLO mac dinh la gi?¶

    A: Tham chieu trong SLA/SLO: uptime, p95 latency, error rate, timeout, quality benchmark.

    Q5. Neu co su co nghiem trong thi xu ly the nao?¶

    A: Kich hoat runbook incident, phan loai P1/P2/P3, cap nhat trang thai va RCA sau su co.

    Q6. Khi nao duoc go-live?¶

    A: Khi dat tieu chi trong go-live checklist va co bien ban sign-off phu hop.

    4. FAQ cho thuong mai/pre-sales¶

    Q7. Co nhung goi dich vu nao?¶

    A: Co 3 goi: Foundation, Professional, Enterprise Readiness. Chi tiet xem bao gia de xuat.

    Q8. Co tai lieu song ngu khong?¶

    A: Co. Da co bo tai lieu song ngu VI-EN cho compliance, pitch, bao gia, huong dan.

    Q9. Co thuoc chuan nao khong?¶

    A: Mo hinh can chinh theo ITIL/ITSM, SRE, ISO 27001/SOC2 control intent, ISO 31000 risk management.

    5. FAQ cho phap ly/chinh sach¶

    Q10. Da co Terms of Service chua?¶

    A: Co, xem Terms of Service.

    Q11. Da co Privacy Policy chua?¶

    A: Co, xem Privacy Policy.

    Q12. Da co Payment Policy chua?¶

    A: Co, xem Payment Policy.

    6. CTA ho tro¶

    • Dat lich assessment: 60 phut
    • Nhan checklist UAT/Go-live
    • Lien he team support qua kenh chinh thuc
