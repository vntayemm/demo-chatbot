    PITCH PACK - DEMO-CHATBOT COMPLIANCE¶

    1. Muc dich¶

    Tai lieu nay tong hop cac phien ban pitch de su dung nhanh theo tung doi tuong khach hang: business, executive, ky thuat, va quoc te.

    2. Danh sach pitch hien co¶

    • Elevator pitch 30s
    • Elevator pitch 30 giay (co ban)
    • Elevator pitch — bien the
    • 2 bien the: Formal tone va Sales tone
    • Elevator pitch — technical buyer
    • Bien the danh cho CTO/CISO
    • Talk track 2 phut
    • Script 2 phut + Q&A nhanh
    • Talk track 5 phut
    • Script 5 phut theo 9 slide + Q&A du phong
    • Compliance statement (executive)
    • Executive summary 1 trang
    • Compliance statement (slide-ready VI)
    • Slide-ready (VI) theo tung slide
    • Compliance statement (slide-ready VI-EN)
    • Slide-ready song ngu VI-EN
    • Compliance statement (VI-EN)
    • Compliance statement song ngu VI-EN day du
    • Meeting template 15 phut
    • Kich ban hop sales/pre-sales 15 phut (agenda + discovery + next step)
    • Kickoff playbook
    • Playbook kick-off sau ky hop dong (agenda + roles + minutes + actions)

    3. Chon pitch theo tinh huong¶

    • Gap lan dau, thoi gian rat ngan (30-60s):
    • Dung elevator pitch 30s
    • Pitch voi business stakeholder (2-5 phut):
    • Dung talk track 2 phut hoac talk track 5 phut
    • Pitch voi C-level (CEO/COO/CFO):
    • Dung compliance statement (executive)
    • Pitch voi CTO/CISO:
    • Dung elevator pitch — technical buyer
    • Pitch voi khach quoc te:
    • Dung slide-ready VI-EN hoac compliance statement VI-EN

    4. Messaging matrix (noi dung can nhan manh)¶

    • Business buyer:
    • Giam rui ro go-live, tang toc do vao production, ROI van hanh
    • Technical buyer (CTO):
    • Reliability, SLO, scalability, observability, MTTR
    • Security buyer (CISO):
    • Access control, auditability, incident response, compliance readiness
    • Executive buyer:
    • Governance, risk control, business continuity, confidence to scale

    5. Core thong diep giu nguyen¶

    • Day khong chi la chatbot demo.
    • Day la chatbot production-ready co governance va operational controls.
    • Co khung do luong (SLA/SLO/KPI), co risk control, co incident response.
    • Muc tieu cuoi: on dinh, minh bach, mo rong ben vung trong doanh nghiep.

    6. Q&A ngan mau dung chung¶

    • Hoi: "Thuoc chuan gi?"
      Dap: "Can chinh theo ITIL/ITSM, SRE, ISO 27001/SOC 2 control intent, va ISO 31000 risk management."

    • Hoi: "Co phai da co chung nhan chinh thuc chua?"
      Dap: "Hien tai la can chinh theo best practices/control intent. Co the mo rong readiness assessment va audit doc lap neu can chung nhan."

    • Hoi: "Do duoc bang gi?"
      Dap: "Bang SLA/SLO va KPI production: uptime, p95 latency, 5xx error rate, va quality benchmark."

    • Hoi: "Neu co su co nghiem trong?"
      Dap: "Da co runbook P1/P2/P3, SLA phan hoi, rollback criteria, va RCA bat buoc sau su co."

    7. De xuat cach su dung cho team sales¶

    • Truoc hop:
    • Chon 1 pitch chinh + 1 pitch backup theo doi tuong.
    • Trong hop:
    • Mo dau bang elevator 30s, sau do mo rong theo script 2-5 phut.
    • Sau hop:
    • Gui file executive hoac bilingual statement theo profile khach hang.

    9. Sales send kit (bao gia + email)¶

    Tai lieu de gui khach nhanh theo tung tinh huong:

    • Bao gia day du
    • Bao gia rut gon 1 trang
    • Bao gia rut gon VI-EN
    • Bao gia VI-EN co USD reference
    • Mail chao gia offers
    • Mail chao gia theo goi A/B/C
    • Mail chao gia A/B/C song ngu

    Goi y su dung nhanh: - Khach noi dia: dung bao gia rut gon 1 trang + mail chao gia theo goi A/B/C - Khach quoc te: dung bao gia VI-EN + USD reference + mail chao gia A/B/C song ngu - Khach can day du governance/compliance: gui them compliance statement VI-EN
