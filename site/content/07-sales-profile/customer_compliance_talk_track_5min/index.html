    TALK TRACK 5 PHUT - CUSTOMER COMPLIANCE PITCH¶

    Huong dan su dung¶

    • Tong thoi luong: 5 phut
    • So slide: 9
    • Cach noi: ngan, ro, tap trung vao gia tri kinh doanh + do tin cay van hanh

    Slide 1 - Enterprise Problem (30s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Demo chatbot khong du de vao production.
    Script goi y:
    "Phan lon chatbot co the demo rat nhanh, nhung khi vao production thi phat sinh 3 van de lon: khong do duoc chat luong, khong co quy trinh xu ly su co, va khong co bang chung van hanh de khach hang tin tuong."

    Slide 2 - Proposed Solution (35s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Chung toi ban giao chatbot kem khung van hanh.
    Script goi y:
    "Giai phap cua chung toi khong chi la chatbot tra loi, ma la chatbot kem bo khung governance: UAT, go-live gate, risk register, SLA/SLO va runbook incident. Nghia la he thong co the release, van hanh va cai tien mot cach co kiem soat."

    Slide 3 - Reference Frameworks (30s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Can chinh theo thong le quoc te.
    Script goi y:
    "Mo hinh nay duoc can chinh theo ITIL/ITSM cho van hanh dich vu, SRE cho do tin cay, ISO 27001/SOC 2 control intent cho bao mat va auditability, va ISO 31000 cho quan tri rui ro."

    Slide 4 - Problems Solved (35s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Moi van de deu co co che xu ly ro rang.
    Script goi y:
    "Go-live rui ro cao duoc giai quyet bang checklist va rollback criteria. Incident duoc xu ly theo cap do P1/P2/P3 de giam MTTR. Nghiem thu duoc chuan hoa bang tieu chi pass/fail de tranh tranh cai ve cam tinh."

    Slide 5 - Operational Commitments (35s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Co cam ket van hanh cu the, khong mo ho.
    Script goi y:
    "Moi release production deu di qua UAT va go-live gate. He thong co SLA/SLO ro rang cho uptime, latency va error rate. Cac rui ro duoc review dinh ky va co owner cu the cho moi hanh dong khac phuc."

    Slide 6 - Production KPIs (35s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Thanh cong duoc do bang so lieu.
    Script goi y:
    "Chi so muc tieu production gom: uptime tu 99.9%, ty le loi 5xx duoi 1%, p95 latency duoi 2 giay, va ty le cau tra loi dung ngu canh tu 85% tro len. Day la nen tang de quan tri theo du lieu, khong theo cam nhan."

    Slide 7 - Customer Value (30s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Loi ich truc tiep cho khach hang.
    Script goi y:
    "Khach hang nhan duoc 3 gia tri ro rang: giam rui ro khi vao production, tang do tin cay dich vu, va tang minh bach trong bao cao van hanh va xu ly su co. Dieu nay giup he thong di duong dai, khong dung lai o muc demo."

    Slide 8 - Market Trend (30s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Day la huong di phu hop xu huong thi truong.
    Script goi y:
    "Tu 2024 den 2026, enterprise AI dang chuyen sang governance-first. Nghia la muon scale thi phai co reliability, observability, security-by-design va auditability. Day chinh la ly do bo tai lieu van hanh nay tro thanh diem cong trong pre-sales."

    Slide 9 - Closing Message (20s)¶

    Thong diep chinh: Khac biet cua giai phap.
    Script goi y:
    "Thong diep cuoi cung: day khong chi la chatbot, day la chatbot production-ready kem khung van hanh va kiem soat. Muc tieu la on dinh, do luong duoc, va mo rong ben vung cho doanh nghiep."

    Q&A du phong (neu con 1-2 phut)¶

    • Hoi: "Day co phai chung nhan ISO/SOC chua?"
      Tra loi: "Hien tai la can chinh theo control intent va best practices; co the mo rong lo trinh de danh gia/chung nhan chinh thuc neu khach hang yeu cau."

    • Hoi: "Bao lau de vao production?"
      Tra loi: "Tuy pham vi, nhung voi bo tai lieu governance co san, thoi gian go-live va rui ro trien khai giam dang ke so voi cach lam chi tap trung vao code."

    • Hoi: "Neu he thong loi thi sao?"
      Tra loi: "Da co runbook P1/P2/P3, SLA phan hoi, rollback criteria, va RCA sau su co de dam bao phuc hoi nhanh va cai tien lien tuc."
