    3. Chan dung khach hang (customer context)¶

    3.1 Hien trang¶

    • Quy trinh hoi dap hien tai dang duoc thuc hien nhu the nao?
    • Van de lon nhat trong van hanh thong tin la gi?
    • Cac kenh nguoi dung hien dang tuong tac (web, Zalo, CRM, helpdesk)?

    3.2 Pain points chinh¶

    • Tra loi cham
    • Noi dung khong dong nhat
    • Khong do duoc chat luong
    • Kho mo rong theo quy mo nguoi dung

    5. Nhom nguoi dung muc tieu¶

    Nhom nguoi dung Nhu cau chinh Muc uu tien
    Sales/CS noi bo Hoi dap nhanh ve gia/huong dan Cao
    Quan ly van hanh Theo doi KPI va tinh on dinh Cao
    IT/Ops De trien khai, de bao tri Trung binh

    6. Yeu cau khach hang theo nhom¶

    6.1 Yeu cau nghiep vu¶

    • Tra loi dung domain (price, guide)
    • Co tinh nhat quan va de hieu
    • Co khuyen nghi hanh dong tiep theo (neu can)

    6.2 Yeu cau ky thuat¶

    • API on dinh
    • Tich hop de dang
    • Co monitoring va alerting

    6.3 Yeu cau bao mat/compliance¶

    • Auth/RBAC (neu production)
    • Bao ve du lieu nhay cam
    • Audit trail va incident response

    7. KPI thanh cong de xuat¶

    KPI nghiep vu¶

    • Ty le cau hoi duoc giai dap dung ngu canh
    • Muc do hai long nguoi dung noi bo
    • Ty le giam tai cho team support

    KPI ky thuat¶

    • Uptime
    • p95 latency
    • 5xx error rate
    • MTTR

    8. Muc do san sang (readiness) cua khach hang¶

    Nhom Trang thai hien tai Ghi chu
    Du lieu/corpus [Ready/Partial/Not ready]
    Team nghiep vu [Ready/Partial/Not ready]
    Team IT/Ops [Ready/Partial/Not ready]
    Governance process [Ready/Partial/Not ready]

    9. Rui ro tu phia khach hang (project risk input)¶

    • Dau moi nghiem thu khong co lich ro rang
    • Corpus chua day du/chua chuan hoa
    • Ky vong scope vuot qua pham vi goi da chot

    Huong giam thieu: - Chot owner + deadline ngay kickoff - Chuan hoa scope va out-of-scope - Dat ky review dinh ky theo sprint/tuan

    10. Ke hoach engagement de xuat¶

    1. Discovery workshop 60-90 phut
    2. Scope & KPI alignment
    3. Proposal + package mapping (A/B/C)
    4. Kickoff va trien khai theo gantt
    5. UAT sign-off -> Go-live sign-off
