    2. Tam nhin va su menh¶

    Tam nhin¶

    Tro thanh doi tac cong nghe tin cay, giup doanh nghiep trien khai AI theo huong an toan, do luong duoc, va mo rong ben vung.

    Su menh¶

    • Bien cac y tuong AI thanh he thong van hanh thuc te.
    • Giam rui ro trien khai va toi uu gia tri kinh doanh.
    • Dong hanh cung khach hang tu giai doan PoC den production.

    3. Nang luc cot loi¶

    • Tu van va trien khai chatbot doanh nghiep
    • Kien truc he thong AI production-ready
    • Governance framework: UAT, Go-live, SLA/SLO, Incident
    • Bao mat va compliance readiness
    • Van hanh va toi uu hieu nang sau go-live

    4. Dich vu chinh¶

    1. AI Chatbot Solutions

      • Price bot, guide bot, support bot
      • API-first chatbot architecture

    2. Delivery & Governance

      • Requirement analysis, SDD, BPMN
      • Project planning (Gantt, RACI, sign-off)

    3. Security & Reliability

      • Security baseline, access control, auditability
      • Reliability setup: KPI, SLO, SLA, runbook

    4. Operations & Support

      • Go-live readiness
      • Hypercare and maintenance
      • Incident response and reporting

    5. Diem khac biet canh tranh¶

    • Khong chi cung cap "chatbot demo", ma cung cap "chatbot production-ready"
    • Co bo tai lieu day du tu pre-sales -> contract -> go-live -> operation
    • Co framework do luong va governance ro rang de cam ket chat luong
    • Kha nang thich ung theo nhieu maturity level (PoC, internal ops, enterprise)

    6. Quy trinh trien khai chuan¶

    1. Assessment & scoping
    2. Architecture & planning
    3. Build & integration
    4. UAT & sign-off
    5. Go-live & hypercare
    6. Ongoing operations & optimization

    7. Nganh/nghiep vu phuc vu¶

    • CRM/Sales
    • Customer Service/Helpdesk
    • Internal Knowledge Management
    • Operations Enablement

    8. Ho so governance va quality¶

    Bo tai lieu da san sang: - UAT checklist - Go-live checklist - Risk register - SLA/SLO policy - Incident runbook - Sign-off templates - Maintenance policy

    9. Mo hinh hop tac¶

    • Goi A - Foundation (PoC nhanh)
    • Goi B - Professional (van hanh noi bo)
    • Goi C - Enterprise Readiness (production scale)

    Co the tuy chinh theo: - Scope nghiep vu - Muc tieu KPI/SLA - Yeu cau bao mat/compliance - Timeline va ngan sach
