RAG la gi va tai sao retrieval quan trong hon prompt trong he thong doanh nghiep

Article 01 · Retrieval Science

Van de khoa hoc can giai

LLM co kha nang sinh ngon ngu tot nhung khong tu dong "biet su that" cua doanh nghiep. Neu khong grounding, model de hallucinates.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

  • Retrieval: tim ngu canh lien quan tu kho tri thuc.
  • Augmentation: chen ngu canh vao prompt.
  • Generation: model tao cau tra loi dua tren ngu canh.

Tai sao retrieval quan trong hon prompt engineering don le

  • Prompt dep khong bu lai duoc du lieu sai/thiếu.
  • Chat luong chatbot phu thuoc vao recall + precision cua retrieval.
  • Trong doanh nghiep, tri thuc thay doi lien tuc -> retrieval linh hoat hon fine-tune lien tuc.

KPI khoa hoc de do retrieval

  • Top-k relevance
  • Grounded answer rate
  • Hallucination proxy rate
  • Latency retrieval stage

Ket luan

Neu muon chatbot dung trong production, hay bat dau tu retrieval pipeline va corpus quality truoc khi toi uu prompt.
