# KICK-OFF PLAYBOOK - Enterprise Chatbot | AI

## 1. Muc tieu buoi kick-off

- Dong bo pham vi da ky va muc tieu du an.
- Chot ke hoach trien khai, milestone, va cach nghiem thu.
- Chot dau moi giao tiep, co che bao cao, va quy trinh xu ly van de.

## 2. Dieu kien truoc kick-off (pre-kickoff checklist)

- [ ] Hop dong va phu luc da ky day du.
- [ ] Bao gia/pham vi final da thong nhat.
- [ ] Da chot goi dich vu (A/B/C hoac custom).
- [ ] Da co danh sach stakeholder 2 ben.
- [ ] Da co draft timeline milestone.
- [ ] Da chuan bi tai lieu trinh bay kick-off.

## 3. Thanh phan tham du de xuat

### Ben A (Khach hang)
- Sponsor/Decision maker
- Product Owner/BA
- Dai dien van hanh (Ops)
- Dai dien ky thuat (IT/Security)

### Ben B (Don vi cung cap)
- Account/Project Lead
- Tech Lead
- AI/Backend Engineer
- DevOps/Support (neu co)

## 4. Agenda kick-off 60-90 phut

1. Gioi thieu thanh phan va vai tro (5 phut)
2. Xac nhan muc tieu kinh doanh va KPI (10 phut)
3. Chot pham vi trien khai va ngoai pham vi (10 phut)
4. Trinh bay kien truc/trien khai va tai lieu van hanh (15 phut)
5. Chot timeline va milestone nghiem thu (15 phut)
6. Chot co che giao tiep, bao cao, risk/escalation (10 phut)
7. Q&A va thong nhat next actions (10-20 phut)

## 5. Noi dung bat buoc phai chot trong kick-off

- [ ] Muc tieu du an va KPI chinh (business + technical).
- [ ] Pham vi in-scope/out-of-scope.
- [ ] Milestone + owner + deadline cho tung giai doan.
- [ ] Tieu chi UAT pass/fail.
- [ ] Dieu kien GO/NO-GO.
- [ ] Co che thay doi pham vi (CR).
- [ ] Nhom lien he su co va escalation path.

## 6. RACI de xuat sau kick-off

- Project Lead (Ben B): dieu phoi tong, bao cao tien do.
- Product Owner (Ben A): xac nhan nghiep vu, phe duyet UAT.
- Tech Lead (Ben B): chiu trach nhiem giai phap ky thuat.
- Ops/IT (Ben A): ho tro moi truong, truy cap, van hanh.
- Security (Ben A/B): ra soat control can thiet truoc go-live.

## 7. Ke hoach giao tiep

- Hop weekly: 1 buoi/tuần (30-60 phut).
- Bao cao tien do: gui truoc hop 24h.
- Kenh van de gap: chat group + escalation call.
- Kenh chia se tai lieu: [Drive/Confluence/Repo].

## 9. Mau email moi hop kick-off (VI)

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Kick-off Meeting] Du an trien khai chatbot production-ready - [Ten Cong Ty]`

**Noi dung mau:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],

Theo ke hoach sau khi ky hop dong, ben em kinh moi Anh/Chi tham du buoi kick-off du an:

- Thoi gian: [Ngay, gio]
- Hinh thuc: [Online/Offline]
- Thanh phan de xuat: PM/PO/Tech/Ops/Security hai ben

Noi dung chinh:
1. Chot pham vi va KPI
2. Chot milestone va ke hoach nghiem thu
3. Chot co che giao tiep, bao cao va escalation

Nho Anh/Chi xac nhan lich hop va thanh phan tham du.

Tran trong,  
[Ten ban]

## 10. Dau ra sau kick-off (24h)

- [ ] Tao lich weekly governance.
- [ ] Chot timeline ban dau tren tracker du an.
- [ ] Xac nhan ngay bat dau thuc thi.
