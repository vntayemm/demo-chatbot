# ELEVATOR PITCH 30 GIAY - FORMAL VA SALES

## 1) Formal tone (doanh nghiep)

### Ban doc 30 giay

> Giai phap chatbot cua chung toi duoc thiet ke theo huong production-ready, ket hop day du cac thanh phan governance va operations control.  
> Mo hinh van hanh can chinh theo ITIL/SRE, bao gom UAT gate, go-live gate, SLA/SLO, va incident runbook theo cap do P1/P2/P3.  
> Cach tiep can nay giup to chuc giam rui ro trien khai, nang cao do tin cay dich vu, va dam bao kha nang mo rong ben vung trong moi truong doanh nghiep.

### One-liner chot

> Khong chi cung cap chatbot, chung toi cung cap khung van hanh co kiem soat cho production.

## 2) Sales tone (thuyet phuc, ngan, manh)

### Ban doc 30 giay

> Ban khong can them mot chatbot demo, ban can mot chatbot co the vao production an toan.  
> Chung toi dong goi san UAT, go-live checklist, SLA/SLO, va runbook su co de he thong van hanh on dinh ngay tu dau.  
> Ket qua la giam rui ro go-live, tang toc do trien khai, va tao niem tin cho khach hang voi cac chi so do luong ro rang.

### One-liner chot

> Demo thi ai cung lam duoc, production-ready co kiem soat moi la khac biet.
