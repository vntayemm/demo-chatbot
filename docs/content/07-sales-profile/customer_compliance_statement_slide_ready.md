# CUSTOMER COMPLIANCE - SLIDE READY

## Slide 1 - Van de cua doanh nghiep

- Chatbot demo chay duoc, nhung kho dua vao production an toan.
- Thieu tieu chi do luong chat luong, do on dinh va xu ly su co.
- Khach hang can bang chung van hanh, khong chi can demo tinh nang.

## Slide 2 - Giai phap de xuat

- Xay chatbot cung bo khung governance va operations readiness.
- Chuan hoa UAT, go-live, risk, SLA/SLO, va incident response.
- Dam bao co the release, van hanh, va cai tien lien tuc.

## Slide 3 - Khung chuan tham chieu

- ITIL/ITSM practices cho incident va release readiness.
- SRE practices cho SLI/SLO/SLA va error budget.
- ISO 27001/SOC 2 control intent cho security va auditability.
- ISO 31000 risk management intent cho quan tri rui ro.

## Slide 4 - Van de duoc giai quyet

- Giam rui ro go-live bang checklist + rollback criteria.
- Tang minh bach van hanh bang metric va dashboard.
- Rut ngan MTTR bang runbook P1/P2/P3.
- Dong nhat nghiem thu bang UAT pass/fail criteria.

## Slide 5 - Cam ket van hanh

- Moi release production deu qua UAT va go-live gate.
- Co SLA/SLO ro rang cho availability, latency, error rate.
- Co risk review dinh ky va owner cho tung action.
- Co quy trinh xu ly su co va RCA sau su co.

## Slide 6 - KPI production de xuat

- Uptime: >= 99.9% / thang.
- API 5xx error rate: <= 1%.
- p95 latency: <= 2.0s (tai tai trong thiet ke).
- Ti le cau tra loi dung ngu canh: >= 85%.

## Slide 7 - Gia tri cho khach hang

- Giam rui ro khi dua chatbot vao quy trinh thuc te.
- Tang do tin cay va kha nang mo rong doanh nghiep.
- Tang kha nang kiem toan, bao cao, va truy vet su co.
- Tao nen tang cho compliance maturity cao hon.

## Slide 8 - Xu huong thi truong

- Enterprise AI dang chuyen sang governance-first.
- Reliability/observability la dieu kien truoc khi scale.
- Security-by-design va auditability la yeu cau mua sam.

## Slide 9 - Thong diep chot

- Day khong chi la chatbot.
- Day la chatbot kem khung van hanh production-ready.
- Muc tieu: on dinh, do luong duoc, va mo rong ben vung.
