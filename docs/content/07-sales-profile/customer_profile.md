## 6. Yeu cau khach hang theo nhom

### 6.1 Yeu cau nghiep vu

- Tra loi dung domain (`price`, `guide`)
- Co tinh nhat quan va de hieu
- Co khuyen nghi hanh dong tiep theo (neu can)

### 6.2 Yeu cau ky thuat

- API on dinh
- Tich hop de dang
- Co monitoring va alerting

### 6.3 Yeu cau bao mat/compliance

- Auth/RBAC (neu production)
- Bao ve du lieu nhay cam
- Audit trail va incident response

## 7. KPI thanh cong de xuat

### KPI nghiep vu

- Ty le cau hoi duoc giai dap dung ngu canh
- Muc do hai long nguoi dung noi bo
- Ty le giam tai cho team support

### KPI ky thuat

- Uptime
- p95 latency
- 5xx error rate
- MTTR
