# TALK TRACK 2 PHUT - CUSTOMER COMPLIANCE PITCH

## Muc tieu

Ban script nay dung cho tinh huong pitch nhanh 2 phut voi stakeholder, tap trung vao 4 y: van de, giai phap, gia tri, va thong diep chot.

## Script 2 phut (goi y doc lien mach)

"Hien nay nhieu chatbot demo chay duoc, nhung khi vao production thuong gap van de: khong do duoc chat luong, khong co quy trinh xu ly su co ro rang, va khong co bang chung van hanh de khach hang tin tuong.

Giai phap cua chung toi khong chi la chatbot, ma la chatbot kem khung governance va operations readiness. Bo khung nay gom UAT checklist, go-live checklist, risk register, SLA/SLO, va incident runbook P1/P2/P3.

Nho do, moi release production deu co gate kiem soat, co rollback criteria, co metric theo doi nhu uptime, latency, error rate va quality, dong thoi co quy trinh ung pho su co de rut ngan thoi gian phuc hoi.

Gia tri cho khach hang la giam rui ro khi go-live, tang do tin cay van hanh, tang minh bach audit, va tao nen tang mo rong ben vung.

Thong diep chot: day khong chi la chatbot tinh nang, day la chatbot production-ready kem khung kiem soat theo thong le quoc te, phu hop xu huong governance-first trong AI doanh nghiep." 

## 3 cau tra loi nhanh khi bi hoi tiep

- **Hoi:** "Thuoc chuan gi?"  
  **Dap:** "Can chinh theo ITIL/ITSM, SRE, ISO 27001/SOC 2 control intent, va ISO 31000 risk management."

- **Hoi:** "Do duoc bang gi?"  
  **Dap:** "Bang SLA/SLO va KPI production: uptime, p95 latency, 5xx error rate, va quality benchmark."

- **Hoi:** "Neu co su co thi sao?"  
  **Dap:** "Da co runbook P1/P2/P3, co SLA phan hoi, rollback criteria, va RCA sau su co."
