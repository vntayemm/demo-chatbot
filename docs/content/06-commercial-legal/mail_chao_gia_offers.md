# MAIL CHAO GIA (OFFERS) - Enterprise Chatbot | AI

## 1. Mau mail chao gia co ban (VI)

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Bao gia] Giai phap Chatbot Production-Ready cho [Ten Cong Ty]`

**Noi dung mail:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],

Cam on Anh/Chi da danh thoi gian trao doi ve nhu cau trien khai chatbot cho [Ten Cong Ty].

Ben em gui Anh/Chi bao gia de xuat cho giai phap `demo-chatbot` theo huong production-ready, bao gom:

- Trien khai `Price bot` + `Guide bot`
- Bo khung van hanh: UAT, go-live checklist, SLA/SLO, risk register, incident runbook
- Ho tro trien khai va van hanh theo goi lua chon

Tai lieu dinh kem/de xuat doc:

- [Bao gia rut gon 1 trang](./bao_gia_rut_gon_1_trang.md)
- [Bao gia de xuat](./bao_gia_de_xuat.md)
- [Compliance statement (executive)](../07-sales-profile/customer_compliance_statement_executive.md)

Neu Anh/Chi dong y, ben em de xuat buoi hop scope 60-90 phut de chot:
1. Goi dich vu phu hop  
2. Timeline trien khai  
3. KPI nghiem thu va van hanh

Tran trong,  
[Ten ban]  
[Chuc danh]  
[Cong ty]  
[So dien thoai] | [Email]

## 2. Mau mail chao gia song ngu

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Quotation] Production-Ready Chatbot Solution for [Company Name]`

**Noi dung mail:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],  
Dear [Name],

Cam on Anh/Chi da trao doi ve nhu cau chatbot cho [Ten Cong Ty].  
Thank you for discussing your chatbot requirements with us.

Ben em gui bao gia de xuat cho giai phap production-ready, bao gom pham vi ky thuat va governance van hanh.  
Please find our proposed quotation for a production-ready chatbot solution, including technical scope and operational governance.

Tai lieu tham chieu:  
Reference documents:

- [Bao gia rut gon VI-EN](./bao_gia_rut_gon_1_trang_bilingual.md)
- [Bao gia VI-EN + USD reference](./bao_gia_rut_gon_1_trang_bilingual_usd_reference.md)
- [Compliance statement VI-EN](../07-sales-profile/customer_compliance_statement_bilingual.md)

Neu phu hop, ben em de xuat lich scope meeting 60-90 phut trong tuan nay de chot pham vi, timeline, va KPI.  
If aligned, we propose a 60-90 minute scoping meeting this week to finalize scope, timeline, and KPIs.

Tran trong / Best regards,  
[Ten ban / Your Name]  
[Chuc danh / Title]  
[Cong ty / Company]  
[Phone] | [Email]

## 3. Mau follow-up sau 2-3 ngay

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Follow-up] Bao gia Chatbot Production-Ready - [Ten Cong Ty]`

**Noi dung mail:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],

Ben em xin follow-up ve bao gia da gui ngay [ngay gui].  
Khong biet Anh/Chi da co thoi gian xem qua chua, va minh co can ben em lam ro them phan nao (pham vi, timeline, chi phi, hoac KPI)?

Neu thuan tien, ben em co the sap xep mot buoi 30 phut de trao doi nhanh va chot next step.

Tran trong,  
[Ten ban]

---

## 4. Mau chot meeting tu mail chao gia

**Subject goi y:**  
`[De xuat lich hop] Chot pham vi trien khai chatbot`

**Noi dung mail:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],

De day nhanh tien do, ben em de xuat buoi hop chot scope theo 2 khung gio:

- [Option 1: Thu, ngay, gio]
- [Option 2: Thu, ngay, gio]

Noi dung hop:
1. Chot goi dich vu  
2. Chot timeline va milestone  
3. Chot KPI UAT/go-live  
4. Chot owner hai ben

Anh/Chi giup xac nhan khung gio phu hop. Ben em se gui meeting invite ngay.

Tran trong,  
[Ten ban]

---

## 5. Checklist truoc khi gui mail chao gia

- [ ] Da gan dung ten khach hang, ten nguoi nhan, va domain bai toan
- [ ] Da chon dung file bao gia (VND hoac VI-EN + USD reference)
- [ ] Da neu ro hieu luc bao gia (30 ngay)
- [ ] Da co CTA ro rang (de xuat lich hop + next step)
- [ ] Da kiem tra chu ky email, so dien thoai, va file dinh kem
