# CHECKLIST KY HOP DONG - Enterprise Chatbot | AI

## 1. Thong tin phap ly bat buoc

- [ ] Ten phap nhan Ben A dung theo dang ky kinh doanh
- [ ] Ten phap nhan Ben B dung theo dang ky kinh doanh
- [ ] Ma so thue, dia chi, nguoi dai dien, chuc vu da dien day du
- [ ] So hop dong va ngay hieu luc da cap nhat
- [ ] Kiem tra tham quyen ky cua nguoi dai dien hai ben

## 2. Kiem tra pham vi va san pham ban giao

- [ ] Da chot goi dich vu (A/B/C hoac custom)
- [ ] Pham vi cong viec da dong bo voi bao gia
- [ ] Danh sach deliverables da ro rang va do duoc
- [ ] Co phan ngoai pham vi (out of scope) de tranh tranh chap
- [ ] Co muc acceptance/UAT criteria ro rang

## 3. Kiem tra thuong mai

- [ ] Tong gia tri hop dong khop bao gia
- [ ] Da xac dinh ro VAT (da/chua bao gom)
- [ ] Lich thanh toan (40/40/20 hoac tuy chinh) da thong nhat
- [ ] Dieu kien xuat hoa don/chung tu da thong nhat
- [ ] Dieu khoan phat cham thanh toan (neu co) da ro rang

## 4. Kiem tra tien do va moc nghiem thu

- [ ] Ngay bat dau va tong thoi gian trien khai da chot
- [ ] Milestones co ngay du kien va dau ra cu the
- [ ] Co quy dinh thoi han phan hoi nghiem thu cua Ben A
- [ ] Co co che xu ly khi cham tien do do phoi hop/du lieu
- [ ] Co co che thay doi pham vi (Change Request)

## 5. Kiem tra bao mat va so huu tri tue

- [ ] Dieu khoan bao mat thong tin da co trong hop dong
- [ ] Quy dinh khong tiet lo cho ben thu ba da ro rang
- [ ] So huu tri tue da xac dinh (IP ownership/license)
- [ ] Quyen su dung san pham ban giao cua Ben A da ro rang
- [ ] Co quy dinh xu ly du lieu nhay cam (neu ap dung)

## 6. Kiem tra van hanh sau ban giao

- [ ] Thoi gian bao hanh ky thuat da dien day du
- [ ] Pham vi bao hanh/khong bao hanh da ro rang
- [ ] Co SLA/SLO va support scope (neu da cam ket)
- [ ] Co quy trinh incident va dau moi lien he sau go-live
- [ ] Co tai lieu ban giao va onboarding cho team van hanh

## 7. Kiem tra dieu khoan phap ly ket thuc

- [ ] Dieu khoan tam ngung/cham dut da ro
- [ ] Dieu khoan boi thuong/phat vi pham da phu hop phap luat
- [ ] Dieu khoan bat kha khang da co
- [ ] Dieu khoan giai quyet tranh chap va toa an tham quyen da ro
- [ ] So ban hop dong, gia tri phap ly tung ban da ghi dung

## 8. Checklist truoc khi gui ky

- [ ] File hop dong dung phien ban cuoi cung (final)
- [ ] Da doi chieu thong tin hop dong voi bao gia va phu luc
- [ ] Da review noi bo (sales + delivery + phap che)
- [ ] Da thong nhat hinh thuc ky (tay/so) va kenh gui
- [ ] Da chuan bi danh sach phu luc dinh kem day du

## 9. Checklist sau khi ky

- [ ] Luu tru hop dong da ky dung thu muc chia se noi bo
- [ ] Gui ban da ky cho cac ben lien quan
- [ ] Tao ticket kickoff va gan owner theo hop dong
- [ ] Tao lich theo doi milestone thanh toan va nghiem thu
- [ ] Cap nhat CRM/tracker trang thai "Contract Signed"
