# BAO GIA RUT GON 1 TRANG - Enterprise Chatbot | AI

## 1. Muc tieu

Trien khai chatbot doanh nghiep theo huong production-ready, bao gom:
- `Price bot` + `Guide bot`
- Bo khung van hanh: UAT, go-live, SLA/SLO, risk, incident runbook
- Ho tro go-live theo goi lua chon

## 2. Goi dich vu va chi phi

| Goi | Pham vi chinh | Thoi gian | Chi phi (VND) |
|---|---|---|---:|
| Foundation | Cai dat co ban + 2 bot + UAT/go-live checklist | 2-3 tuan | 120,000,000 |
| Professional | Foundation + risk/SLA-SLO/runbook + benchmark + onboarding | 4-6 tuan | 240,000,000 |
| Enterprise Readiness | Professional + hardening + compliance readiness + hypercare | 8-10 tuan | 420,000,000 |

## 3. Dich vu van hanh tuy chon (hang thang)

- Goi co ban: **25,000,000 VND/thang** (ho tro P2/P3 gio hanh chinh)
- Goi nang cao: **45,000,000 VND/thang** (theo doi 24/7 + ho tro P1/P2/P3)

## 4. Dieu khoan thuong mai

- Gia tren **chua bao gom VAT**.
- Thanh toan de xuat: `40% ky hop dong - 40% sau UAT - 20% khi go-live/ban giao`.
- Hieu luc bao gia: **30 ngay** ke tu ngay phat hanh.

## 5. Gia dinh va ngoai pham vi

- Khach hang cung cap dau moi nghiep vu va phe duyet corpus.
- Khong bao gom chi phi ha tang va license ben thu ba.
- Khong bao gom phi chung nhan compliance chinh thuc.
- Tich hop dac thu he thong ngoai se bao gia rieng.

## 6. De xuat next step

- Buoc 1: Hop scope 60-90 phut de chot goi phu hop.
- Buoc 2: Xac nhan pham vi va timeline chi tiet.
- Buoc 3: Kickoff trien khai trong 3-5 ngay lam viec.
