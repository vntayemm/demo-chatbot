# CONTRACT SIGNING COVER EMAIL - Enterprise Chatbot | AI

## 1. Mau email gui hop dong ky chinh thuc (VI)

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Hop dong ky] Dich vu trien khai chatbot production-ready - [Ten Cong Ty]`

**Noi dung email:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],

Cam on Anh/Chi da thong nhat pham vi va dieu khoan thuong mai cho du an chatbot.

Ben em gui Anh/Chi bo ho so ky chinh thuc gom:

- Hop dong dich vu: `[Ten file hop dong]`
- Phu luc 01 (Scope of Work): `[Ten file]`
- Phu luc 02 (Milestones): `[Ten file]`
- Phu luc 03 (UAT/Acceptance): `[Ten file]`
- [Cac phu luc khac neu co]

Kinh nho Anh/Chi ho tro:

1. Kiem tra thong tin phap ly (ten don vi, MST, nguoi dai dien, dia chi).  
2. Xac nhan noi dung pham vi va lich thanh toan.  
3. Ky/xac nhan theo hinh thuc [ky so/ky tay] truoc [thoi gian du kien].

Sau khi nhan ban ky hop le, ben em se:

- Xac nhan hieu luc hop dong;
- Gui ke hoach kickoff chi tiet;
- Kich hoat cac dau viec trien khai theo milestone.

Tran trong,  
[Ten ban]  
[Chuc danh] - [Cong ty]  
[So dien thoai] | [Email]

## 2. Mau email gui hop dong ky

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Contract for Signature] Production-Ready Chatbot Services - [Company Name]`

**Noi dung email:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],  
Dear [Name],

Ben em gui bo hop dong ky chinh thuc cho du an chatbot theo noi dung da thong nhat.  
Please find the contract package for signature, as aligned in our previous discussions.

Ho so dinh kem / Attached documents:

- Service Contract: `[File name]`
- Appendix 01 (Scope of Work): `[File name]`
- Appendix 02 (Milestones): `[File name]`
- Appendix 03 (UAT/Acceptance): `[File name]`
- [Other appendices if applicable]

Nho Anh/Chi ho tro kiem tra va ky truoc [date].  
Kindly review and sign by [date].

Sau khi nhan ban ky hop le, ben em se gui ke hoach kickoff va timeline trien khai chi tiet.  
Upon receiving the duly signed copy, we will send the detailed kickoff plan and implementation timeline.

Tran trong / Best regards,  
[Ten ban / Your Name]  
[Title] - [Company]  
[Phone] | [Email]

## 3. Mau follow-up nhac ky sau 2 ngay

**Subject goi y:**  
`[Follow-up] Hop dong ky du an chatbot - [Ten Cong Ty]`

**Noi dung email:**

Kinh gui Anh/Chi [Ten],

Ben em xin phep follow-up ve bo hop dong ky da gui ngay [ngay gui].  
Khong biet Anh/Chi da xem qua va co can ben em ho tro/chinh sua thong tin nao truoc khi ky khong?

Neu thuan tien, ben em xin de xuat moc ky truoc [ngay] de dam bao tien do kickoff theo ke hoach.

Tran trong,  
[Ten ban]

---

## 4. Checklist truoc khi bam gui email hop dong

- [ ] Dung file hop dong phien ban final
- [ ] Day du cac phu luc da thong nhat
- [ ] Da dien thong tin phap ly cua 2 ben
- [ ] Da neu ro han ky/xac nhan
- [ ] Da ghi ro hinh thuc ky (ky so/ky tay)
- [ ] Da co nguoi nhan CC noi bo (sales, delivery, phap che)
