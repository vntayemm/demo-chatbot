# BLOG 05 - KPI, SLO, SLA: BO 3 BAT BUOC KHI VAN HANH CHATBOT

## Mo dau

Neu khong do duoc, ban khong the cai tien chatbot.  
Voi doanh nghiep, KPI - SLO - SLA la bo 3 de bien chatbot thanh dich vu co the cam ket.

## KPI la gi?

KPI la chi so kinh doanh/van hanh de danh gia hieu qua:

- Ty le cau hoi duoc tra loi dung ngu canh
- Ty le giam tai cho team CS/Sales
- Muc do hai long nguoi dung

## SLO la gi?

SLO la muc tieu ky thuat noi bo:

- p95 latency <= nguong muc tieu
- 5xx error rate <= nguong cho phep
- timeout rate <= nguong cho phep

## SLA la gi?

SLA la cam ket dich vu voi khach hang:

- Uptime theo thang
- Thoi gian phan hoi su co P1/P2/P3
- Muc tieu khoi phuc

## Vi sao can dong bo ca 3?

- KPI cho biet chatbot co tao gia tri khong
- SLO cho biet he thong co on dinh khong
- SLA cho biet co the cam ket voi khach hang khong

Neu thieu mot trong ba, chatbot de roi vao trang thai:
"van chay, nhung khong ai chac la dang chay tot."

## Ket luan

Khi chatbot duoc quan tri bang KPI/SLO/SLA, doi ngu moi co co so de quyet dinh mo rong hay toi uu.

**CTA:** Dat lich 60 phut de setup bo KPI/SLO/SLA phu hop voi muc tieu doanh nghiep cua ban.
