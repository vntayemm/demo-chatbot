# BLOG 01 - VI SAO CHATBOT DEMO KHONG DU CHO DOANH NGHIEP

## Mo dau

Nhieu chatbot co the demo rat nhanh, nhung khi dua vao production lai gap 3 van de lon:

- Chat luong khong on dinh
- Khong co quy trinh xu ly su co
- Khong do luong duoc hieu qua

## Van de cot loi

Demo thuong chi tra loi cau hoi dep trong dieu kien ly tuong.  
Production can:

- Tai cao
- Nguoi dung that
- Loi bat ngo
- Yeu cau bao mat va truy vet

Neu khong co governance, chatbot de roi vao tinh trang:

- "luc dung luc sai"
- kho mo rong
- kho bao cao cho lanh dao

## Chuyen tu demo sang production-ready can gi?

1. **UAT checklist** de nghiem thu co tieu chi
2. **Go-live checklist** de kiem soat rui ro trien khai
3. **SLA/SLO** de do uptime, latency, error rate
4. **Runbook incident** de xu ly P1/P2/P3 nhanh
5. **Risk register** de quan tri rui ro dinh ky

## Loi ich cho doanh nghiep

- Giam rui ro go-live
- Tang do tin cay cua he thong
- Co KPI ro rang de danh gia hieu qua
- De mo rong theo team, khong phu thuoc 1 ca nhan

## Ket luan

Chatbot demo giup kiem chung y tuong.  
Chatbot production-ready moi giup doanh nghiep van hanh ben vung.

**CTA:** Dat lich buoi assessment 60 phut de xac dinh muc do san sang production cho chatbot cua ban.
