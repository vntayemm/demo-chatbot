## Post 02 - Checklist truoc go-live

Truoc khi go-live chatbot, team ban da check 5 muc nay chua?

1) UAT pass  
2) KPI ky thuat dat nguong  
3) Bao mat da bat  
4) Rollback plan ro rang  
5) Runbook P1/P2/P3 san sang

Mot checklist dung co the cuu ca du an.

## Post 04 - Bao mat va compliance

Bao mat chatbot khong phai "optional".  
Can co toi thieu 5 lop:

- TLS/HTTPS
- Auth + RBAC
- Secret/Data protection
- Audit log
- Incident response

Bao mat khong lam cham du an, bao mat giup du an song lau.

## Post 05 - KPI SLO SLA

Khi nao chatbot duoc goi la "dich vu"?

Khi ban do duoc:

- KPI (gia tri kinh doanh)
- SLO (muc tieu ky thuat)
- SLA (cam ket dich vu)

Khong do duoc = khong cai tien duoc.
