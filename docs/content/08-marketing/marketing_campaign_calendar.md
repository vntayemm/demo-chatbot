## 4. KPI campaign de xuat

### KPI top-funnel

- Blog views
- Social reach/impressions
- Email open rate
- Email click-through rate

### KPI mid-funnel

- So luong lead moi
- So lead dat meeting
- Tyle convert blog -> lead

### KPI bottom-funnel

- So buoi assessment/workshop
- So proposal/bao gia gui
- So co hoi chuyen sang dam phan hop dong

## 5. Rule van hanh campaign

- Moi bai viet phai co CTA ro rang.
- Moi lead tu content phai duoc sales follow-up trong 24h.
- Weekly review (30 phut): update KPI + dieu chinh thong diep.
- Neu open rate email < nguong muc tieu, A/B test lai subject.
