Do luong chatbot bang KPI, SLO, SLA: tu cam tinh sang van hanh theo du lieu

KPI, SLO, SLA khac nhau nhu the nao?

  • KPI: gia tri kinh doanh (adoption, deflection, CSAT).
  • SLO: muc tieu ky thuat noi bo (p95, 5xx, timeout).
  • SLA: cam ket dich vu voi khach hang.

Mo hinh do luong de xuat cho chatbot

  • Layer 1 - Reliability: uptime, error, latency.
  • Layer 2 - Quality: grounded rate, fallback/handoff rate.
  • Layer 3 - Business: conversion, support deflection.

Error budget va ra quyet dinh

Khi tieu hao error budget nhanh, uu tien on dinh he thong truoc khi roll out tinh nang moi. Day la nguyen tac cot loi cua SRE.

Ket luan

Chatbot khong do duoc la chatbot khong the cai tien co he thong. KPI/SLO/SLA la bo 3 bien he thong AI thanh dich vu co the cam ket.
