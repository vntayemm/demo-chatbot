Bao mat va compliance cho chatbot doanh nghiep

Blog 04 • Security • 4 phut doc

Bao mat chatbot can duoc thiet ke ngay tu dau, khong phai bo sung sau.

  • TLS/HTTPS cho toan bo kenh truyen.
  • Auth + RBAC theo role nguoi dung.
  • Khong log token/PII, quan ly secret an toan.
  • Audit log + monitoring + alerting.
  • Runbook incident + RCA sau su co.

Compliance readiness la kha nang map control, co bang chung, va co quy trinh review dinh ky.
