Threat model cho chatbot doanh nghiep: cac vector tan cong va bien phap phong thu

Cac vector tan cong pho bien

  • Prompt injection va instruction override.
  • Data leakage qua logs/sources.
  • Abuse API (spam, brute force, cost attack).
  • Privilege misuse khi thieu auth/RBAC.

Control map thuc dung

  • Transport security: TLS.
  • Access security: JWT/OAuth2 + RBAC.
  • Input/output guardrails: sanitize + policy checks.
  • Observability: security logs + anomaly alerts.
  • Incident response: P1/P2/P3 runbook + RCA.

Nguyen tac "defense-in-depth"

Khong co 1 lop bao ve nao du. He thong chatbot can nhieu lop bao ve doc lap tu network, app, data den operations process.

Ket luan

Threat model khong phai tai lieu de co. Day la cong cu de quyet dinh uu tien security ngay trong thiet ke va van hanh hang ngay.
