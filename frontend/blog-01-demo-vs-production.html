Vi sao chatbot demo khong du cho doanh nghiep

Blog 01 • Governance-first • 5 phut doc

Mo dau

Nhieu chatbot demo rat nhanh, nhung vao production lai gap su co lien tuc: tra loi khong on dinh, khong do duoc chat luong, va khong co quy trinh xu ly loi.

Van de cot loi

  • Demo thuong chi dung trong dieu kien ly tuong.
  • Production doi hoi tai cao, du lieu that, va nguoi dung that.
  • Khong governance = khong scale ben vung.

Can gi de production-ready

  • UAT checklist + sign-off
  • Go-live checklist + rollback criteria
  • SLO/SLA va KPI van hanh
  • Runbook incident P1/P2/P3
  • Risk register va owner ro rang

Ket luan

Chatbot demo de kiem chung y tuong. Chatbot production-ready moi tao gia tri thuc cho doanh nghiep.
