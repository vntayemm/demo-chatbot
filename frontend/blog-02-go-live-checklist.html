Blog 02 • Go-live readiness • 4 phut doc

Go-live thanh cong den tu ky luat checklist, khong den tu may man.

  • UAT pass, khong con loi Sev1.
  • KPI ky thuat dat nguong (p95, 5xx, timeout).
  • Bao mat da bat (TLS, auth, RBAC, audit).
  • Rollback plan va owner ro rang.
  • Runbook incident va on-call san sang.

Neu chua dat 1 muc critical, de xuat NO-GO va lui lich de giam rui ro.
